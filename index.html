DNA customs was founded in 2020 by Andrew Gorman. DNA customs specialize in custom-fitted golf clubs. With our expert service, you can help alter your game and look good in the process. Here at DNA, we are passionate about Custom clubs, we work to the highest standards to ensure your clubs are customized to perfection to help cut shots off your handicap. We will help peak your performance on the golf course with our services which include re-gripping, club repairs, stamping, and paintfall.

Andrew Gorman

Andrew is a Professional Golfer and PGA coach. He is the Assistant PGA Professional at Killeen Castle Golf Club located in Meath. Andrew grew up playing golf from an early age in Ashbourne Golf Club where he spent his weekends and summers developing his love and passion for the game of golf there. Andrew started tweaking and perfecting his clubs’ grips and shafts from a young age and soon started fixing his friends and family’s clubs. When he started working in Killeen Castle, he soon started helping members customize their clubs and improve their all-round game.
